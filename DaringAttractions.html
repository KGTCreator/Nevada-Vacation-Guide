Daring Attractions

We already know that you came to have some fun. Weather you are the oldest adult, or a kid that loves life. We have everything just for you, all of you!

The Strat

Formally known as The Stratosphere, this resort is a combination of a hotel, casino, and theme park! This place is best known for the four attractions at the top of their tower. Insanity, X-Scream, Big Shot, and Sky Jump. Besides that, the hotel also has a casino and restaurants that are sure to keep you and your family entertained!

Area 15

You are not reading that title wrong. Area 15 is a whole building dedicated for the wildest, wierdest, and woe enducing attractions that creative minds have to offer. The Immersive Van Gogh Expierence is now open and ready to explore. Alongside ziplines, VR Rooms, and an entire emersive playground for all ages! But the Main Attraction is MeowWolf's Omega Mart! A creepy supermarket with dark secrets!

Great Baison National Park

Without questuion, one of the best places to go in Nevada doesn't need to have flashing lights, crazy rides, or mind bending sights. Sometimes the best attractions are that of Nature. Great Baison is the best place to be for all of this! This park has so many sights to see, including the mountains, great desert, and wildlife that roams free! It is the perfect place to unwind and connect to nature in all of it's glory!
