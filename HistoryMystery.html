History Mystery

You can't enter Nevada without knowing where it came from. So this page is here to help with that.

The Begining

Nevada had started out as small settlements by Native Americans, and European Missionaries before it's border could be drawn.

During the US Mexican War, most of this territory belonged to the Mexican Monarchy. The Americans had later turned into a Free State thanks to the Wilmot Proviso.

By the time of the Gold Rush, prospecters had rushed into Nevada to gain all the gold they could. Some succeded, others did not.

Despite location, Nevada was added to the Union, after the Confederacy had split from America and made their own governemnt.

The Present

Today, Nevada is best known for Las Vegas, the central place of the rich and gambling. Along with theatrics of all kinds if lucky.

This place is also the set of a famous wed series, Madness Combat. Lets be glad that this is not what Nevada is like in real life.

Nevada is one of the few states to have a desert. Not a major one however, but it still counts.
